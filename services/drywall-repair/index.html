Drywall Repair FAQ

Can you match existing texture?

We can handle many common textures and will set expectations before work begins if the match may be visible.

Do I need to paint after drywall repair?

Most drywall repairs need primer and paint after the patch dries. We can discuss paint-ready prep during the estimate.

Can you repair small holes?

Yes. Small holes, dents, nail pops and anchor damage are exactly the kind of handyman repairs we handle.
